Remotley Operated UnderWater Vehicle

This project consisted of a team of 5 students pioneering the first ever team of this sort at UCI. One of the other students and I had worked on leading this team to success by organizing team meeting times and assigning the other members with a component to better manage progress. The team had begun with an intricate design process to analyze requirements provided by Dr. Hassaan while also being cost effective and efficient. We were able to brainstorm many different aspects of this including a camera for visualization, motors for propulsion, a controller to control direction and acension and decension, and a buoyancy system to allow for neutral buoyancy. Although this product is in testing phase the components have been selected and fit the criteria to be a succesful ROV.
