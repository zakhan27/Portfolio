From Hobby to Professional Venture

ZDroneProductions began as a hobby after I purchased my first drone, a DJI Mini 2. I would take it along wherever I went, recording footage of my travels and experiences. Leveraging my previous video editing skills, I would add soundtracks and seamlessly mesh together the footage to create compelling stories. Sharing these videos with family and friends, I received overwhelming praise for the footage quality and editing, revealing a market for these skills. To publicize my brand, I started a TikTok page and networked whenever an opportunity arose. Before long, KNEX, a software consultancy, reached out to me to provide drone footage for a gala opening video. Additionally, I collaborated with the Muslim Soccer League associated with my local mosque, successfully shooting and editing a promotional video for their men's soccer league.
